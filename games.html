About the game

FunEasyLearn – Learn Slovak

FunEasyLearn offers a comprehensive and gamified approach to learning Slovak. The app provides access to 6,000 Slovak words and 5,000 phrases, all accompanied by hand-drawn illustrations and high-quality audio recordings. Learners can engage with 30 interactive games designed to enhance vocabulary, pronunciation, reading, writing, and listening skills. The content is organised into 350 topics and 10 difficulty levels, catering to beginners through advanced learners. Additional features include a smart review manager, progress tracking, and offline accessibility, making it a versatile tool for structured language learning.

Digital Dialects – Slovak Games

Digital Dialects provides free, browser-based games aimed at beginners learning Slovak. The platform focuses on essential vocabulary topics such as numbers, colours, animals, and basic phrases. Each game is interactive and designed for quick learning sessions, making it suitable for both children and adults. The games are compatible with desktops, tablets, and mobile devices, offering an accessible and enjoyable way to reinforce Slovak vocabulary and pronunciation.
